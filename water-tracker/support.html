App overview

Water Tracker helps you record hydration, monitor progress toward a daily goal, and stay consistent with a simple, focused experience.

  • Quick logging of water intake
  • Progress toward your goal
  • Data stored on your device (see Privacy Policy for analytics and ads)
  • Clean, easy-to-use interface
